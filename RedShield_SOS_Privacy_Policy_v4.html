1. Privacy Policy

1.1 We Do Not Collect Your Data

RedShield SOS does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything you enter in the app — emergency contacts, medical notes, PIN, settings — stays only on your device. Nothing is sent to us or any server.

1.2 What Is Stored on Your Device

  • Your ICE (emergency) contacts and phone numbers
  • Medical or profile information you choose to add
  • Your Master PIN
  • App settings (shake sensitivity, language, etc.)
  • A local log of past SOS events (for your own reference)

None of this is ever shared with us or anyone else. The app has no analytics, no ads, no accounts, and no internet connection to our servers.

1.3 How SOS Works

When an SOS is triggered, the app reads your GPS location on your device and sends an SMS from your own SIM card to your chosen contacts. This is similar to you sending a text message — it goes through your mobile network only. We are not involved in this process in any way.

1.4 Permissions the App Uses

  • SMS: To send emergency alerts to your contacts
  • Location: To include your GPS coordinates in the SOS message
  • Camera (torch only): To turn on your flashlight as a signal
  • Vibration: For alert patterns
  • Run in background: To keep shake and stroke detection active even when the screen is off
  • Start on boot: To automatically resume monitoring after a restart
  • Notifications: To show the monitoring status notification required by Android
  • Bluetooth (optional): To allow a Bluetooth button to trigger SOS

All permissions are used only for the features described above. No data from these permissions is ever sent to us.

1.5 Deleting Your Data

Uninstalling the app removes all your data automatically. You can also clear your data from within the app at any time. We have no access to your data and cannot delete or recover it remotely.
